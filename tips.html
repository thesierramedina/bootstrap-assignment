T R A V E L O S O P H Y

experiencing life freely

We think we want to get the most bang for our buck by painstakingly planning out every little detail, but we lose so much in traveling when we do that. The thrill and anticipation from a spontaneous trip are like no other, however there are some practical ways to better prepare yourself to experience life more freely.

Although Travelosophy plans your flights and lodging, or connections with guides and translators depending on the location, we don’t do much else. That’s because we want you to experience the whimsy of unplanned travel. Below you will find some helpful tips for beginners and even those who know a little more:

  1. Be spontaneous! Follow an interesting sign and change your plan.
  2. Get lost on purpose! This is the best way to come across unexpected treasures.
  3. Try to plan only one major attraction a day, choose one absolute must see and let everything else be through serendipity.
  4. Check the local weeklies for festivals, concerts, classes, or anything else that the community might be offering. How better to experience what a local would!
  5. Better yet, ask a local! They will be pretty much everywhere and will know what is mundane and what is spectacular.
  6. Have an unplanned day!
